    So with the 17th Anniversary coming up soon and, from my understanding, the event being tied with the BC in some way… I got to wondering if we could end up returning to Outland in some manner in the future. Like we end up with another version of Outland with the Dark Portal opening all Void-like and we head in to see that the Void staked a claim on the shattered realm and we realign with those left behind there to help beat it back.
